recipe for love


what you'll need

    attraction
    tenderness
    understanding
    sweetness
    friendship (optional)


materials


    a meetcut

*it's a simple one pot recipe!

cook time:


min = at first site
max = many, many years

directions


  • your pot should be pre-seasoned with attraction
  • add a lot of tenderness. perhaps more than you use with usual recipes, as this one is special. if you need it to be more tender, feel free to butterfly it.
  • add about 3 cups of understanding. this is one of the most important ingredients. once you've added understanding bring the heat down to medium low and let simmer for 5 hours.
  • add sweetness to taste. also feel free to improvise how much sweetness depending on who you're making it for. some like only a dash, whereas others may like a lot more.
  • some of the best love comes from friendships. however, it's not necessary for this recipe. only use friendhsip if you feel so inclined to or it comes naturally to you.
